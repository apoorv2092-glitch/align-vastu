Align Vastu — Privacy Policy

वास्तु शास्त्र · Your home energy guide

Last updated: April 2025

Align Vastu ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

1. Information We Collect

Align Vastu is designed to work entirely on your device. We collect only the information you voluntarily enter during the app experience:

  • House type and entrance direction (used to calculate your Vastu score)
  • Selected home zones and primary concerns
  • Remedy completion progress and streak count
  • Saved remedies and daily action status
  • Notification preferences (on/off)
All data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your data is used solely to provide the features of the app:

  • Calculate and display your personalised House Energy Score
  • Show your daily Vastu remedy and track your streak
  • Deliver local daily reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • Remember your saved remedies across sessions

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is described above.

3. Data Storage & Security

All personal data you provide is stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. We do not operate servers that store your personal information. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data.

4. Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, the app schedules local notifications directly on your device. These notifications are generated entirely on-device — no data is sent to any server to deliver them. You can disable notifications at any time from the app's Settings or from your device's system notification settings.

5. AI Chat Feature (Coming Soon)

A future version of Align Vastu will include an AI-powered chat feature. When this feature becomes available, messages you send may be processed by a third-party AI provider to generate responses. This Privacy Policy will be updated before that feature is released to describe exactly how your data is handled.

6. Third-Party Services

The current version of Align Vastu does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not share your data with any third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Align Vastu is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

8. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control over it at all times:

  • Reset your Vastu profile and score from Settings → Retake Assessment
  • Delete all app data by uninstalling Align Vastu from your device

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
